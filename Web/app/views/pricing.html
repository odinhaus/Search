Simple Payments

Our model is simple: pay for what you use.

All payments are billed to the credit/debit card of your choice. Charges are posted at the end of every month, based on cumulative service utilization as described above. You may cancel at any time. You may change your method of payment at any time.

And if you never want to pay, that's ok too. Just choose to restrict your service to never incur charges when you register your account, and you'll never even need to give us a credit card.

That's it. It doesn't get much easier than that.

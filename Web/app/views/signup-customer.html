Suffuz requires your organization to register using your Slack team identity. This will allow you to authorize us to provide content to your Slack channels. You can remove the authorization at any time.

* You will be taken to slack.com to authorize suffuz.com to have access to certain aspects of your slack team. Once you have authorized suffuz.com to access your slack team, you will be redirected back to complete the registration process.
